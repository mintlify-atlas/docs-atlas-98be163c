DirectPackageInstaller (DPI) is a cross-platform desktop tool that lets you send PKG files to your PS4 or PS5 without copying files manually. Paste a URL, click Install, and DPI handles everything — resolving file hosting links, proxying downloads over your LAN, and communicating with your console's package installer.

## How it works

DPI runs a local HTTP server on your PC and acts as a bridge between a PKG source (direct URL, debrid service, or compressed archive) and your console's package installer. It supports three installation methods:

- **Remote Package Installer homebrew** — communicates via the RPI HTTP API at port 12800
- **GoldHEN Payload Server** — sends a payload to ports 9090, 9021, or 9020
- **etaHEN DPIv2 API** — sends packages via multipart POST to port 12800

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare your console">
    Launch Remote Package Installer, enable the GoldHEN Payload Server, or start etaHEN on your PS4 or PS5.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open DirectPackageInstaller">
    Run DPI on your PC, Mac, Linux machine, or Android device. Make sure both devices are on the same network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste a PKG URL">
    Paste a direct `.pkg` link, a debrid-service link, a compressed archive URL, or a PSN update manifest URL into the PKG URL field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load and Install">
    Click **Load** to fetch package metadata, then click **Install** to push it to your console.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Automatic proxy" icon="network-wired">
    DPI proxies downloads through your PC over LAN, significantly improving speeds for consoles with slow WAN connections.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compressed archive support" icon="file-zipper">
    RAR and 7z files are downloaded, extracted, and streamed to your console in parallel — no extra disk space needed upfront.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Segmented downloads" icon="gauge-high">
    Download PKG files in parallel threads for maximum speed before pushing to your console.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Debrid services" icon="link">
    Integrated support for AllDebrid, RealDebrid, and DebridLink — just add your API key in settings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="NFS source" icon="server">
    Point DPI at an NFS server export and install PKGs directly from your NAS.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cross-platform" icon="laptop">
    Runs on Windows, Linux, macOS, and Android. macOS builds are self-contained with the .NET runtime bundled.
  </Card>
